NEVSKY Second Edition – Changes Summary                                  7 June 2023
 
 
LORD MATS
=========
Hermann and Knud & Abel: Forces replace 1x Men-at-Arms with 1x Militia.
Knud & Abel: Add Coin x1.
 
 
FOLDOUT PAC
===========
Commands: Update Supply, Ravage, and Supply Sources and Routes summaries per Rules of Play below.
Forces: Knights – Add helmet Armor icon to counter image. (Correct Men-at-Arms Melee to "x1".)
Strongholds: Bishopric and Castle Garrisons – reduce each by 1x Men-at-Arms.
Battle and Storm: Update Storm Procedure Strike to max 6 Hits/Lord in Melee.
 
 
TEUTONS AND RUS PACs
====================
Update Hermann and Knud & Abel Forces and Coin per Lord Mats above. Remove Andrey exception.
 
 
RULES OF PLAY
=============
Arts of War: Each time after drawing a No Event/No Capability card, remove that card from play.
 
Disband: Count cylinder placement from next box if Disbanding during Campaign.
 
Muster and Call to Arms - delete Andrey exception, improve Rus cylinder shift.
 
Vassal Service Advanced Rule: Place Vassal on Calendar only if to left of that Lord's Service marker.
 
Storm: Update to Maximum 6 Melee Hits per Lord per new L&C standard.
 
Supply: Update to 1x Transport/Provender (no double duty on multiple Sources) per new L&C standard.
 
Ravage: Modify to use 2x actions if Unbesieged Enemy Lord is adjacent to the target Locale.
 
End of Campaign: End of each Rasputista (turns 8 & 16), Grow to remove Ravage to ½ total markers.
 
Scenarios: Add a Bidding for sides option, similar to that in Almoravid.
 
Pleskau – Special Rule: Remove all "No Event" Arts of War cards before play.
 
Watland – Updated Lords setup. Special Rule: Victory for Teutons requires 2x Russian VP and reaching 7 VP.
 
Return of the Prince – Gavrilo sets up box 9.
 
Crusade on Novgorod – Andreas sets up box 3; Special: Keep "No" cards.
 
Correct errata and clarify various rules text (such as Battle Array, Relief Sally, and Flanking).
 
 
BACKGROUND BOOK
===============
Add Quickstart Scenario setup, Battle mini-game.
Correct errata, update to Second Edition rules, and clarify various text.
 
 
NOT INCLUDED IN UPDATE KIT MATERIALS—
=====================================
Countersheets: Replace 12x Siege and Conquered with two added sets of "1" to "6" number markers.
Add helmet Armor icon to Knights counter image.
 
Forces Pieces: Lighter gray steel Sergeants and Men-at-Arms, duller buff Asiatic Horse and tan Serfs.
Replace 2x Men-at-Arms with 2x Militia (to account for Hermann and Knud & Abel Forces changes above).
 
Gameboard: Add tiny green "Ravaged to ½ (round up)" at lower right corner of Calendar.
